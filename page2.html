Candles

ITEMS NEEDED:

  1. Wick
  2. Scented Oils
  3. Parrafin Wax
  4. Coloring
  5. Spoon

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Take your wax, chop it up into shreds and pieces and put it in a pot of water to melt while it's in a heat resistant container in.
  2. Start mixing the melting wax with your spoon.
  3. Add your coloring to the wax as it mixes.
  4. Take your wick and put it into a candle mold and wrap it on a stick and have the top stick out as the other part rests at the bottom of the candle mold.
  5. Pour the candle wax into the mold and let cool.
  6. Once cooled you now have your candle and can enjoy the sweet DIY candle!

    7. Slime

    ITEMS NEEDED:

    1. Glue
    2. Water
    3. Borax Powder
    4. Food Coloring
    5. Spoon
    6. Bowls

    DIRECTIONS:

    1. Mix some borax with water in one bowl and glue with water in another bowl.
    2. Mix the bowls together and stir til substance turns thicker.
    3. Add coloring to mix.
    4. ENJOY YOUR SLIME!
